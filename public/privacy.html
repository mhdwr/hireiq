Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 2026  ·  Last Updated: May 2026

At HireIQ, your privacy matters. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you use HireIQ:

  • Account data: Your email address and authentication details when you sign up
  • Uploaded content: Job descriptions and CV files you upload to the platform
  • Usage data: How you interact with the platform (pages visited, features used)
  • Device data: Browser type, IP address, and operating system for security purposes

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Provide and improve the HireIQ CV screening service
  • Authenticate your account and keep it secure
  • Process and score uploaded CVs against job descriptions
  • Send important account notifications (e.g., email verification)
  • Respond to support requests

We do not sell your data to third parties. We do not use uploaded candidate CVs for any purpose other than generating screening results for you.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your account data is stored securely using Firebase (Google Cloud infrastructure). Uploaded files are processed in real-time and are not permanently stored on our servers beyond what is necessary to generate your results.

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no system is 100% secure — please use a strong, unique password for your account.

4. Candidate Data Responsibility

When you upload CVs of job applicants, you are acting as a data controller for that candidate information. You are responsible for ensuring you have the legal right to process and upload that data, and that doing so complies with applicable privacy laws in your region.

5. Third-Party Services

HireIQ uses the following third-party services to operate:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication and data storage
  • Resend: Transactional email delivery
  • Vercel: Platform hosting and deployment
  • OpenAI / AI APIs: CV analysis and scoring

Each of these services has its own privacy policy and security practices.

6. Cookies

HireIQ uses minimal cookies necessary for authentication and session management. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Withdraw consent at any time by deleting your account

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at harisdawar008@gmail.com.

8. Children's Privacy

HireIQ is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or a notice on the platform. Continued use after changes are posted means you accept the updated policy.
